VS Forms is a specialized form solution designed for USA-based travel agent advisors, from owner-operators to small and medium agencies.

## Get Started

VS Forms offers a streamlined approach to creating professional forms for travel advisors. With our platform, you can easily create client onboarding forms, travel preference questionnaires, trip planning forms, feedback collection forms, and travel itinerary confirmations.

## Key Features

-   🚀 Travel-specific templates for immediate use
-   📝 Various input types: Text, Date, URL, File uploads & much more
-   🌐 Embed forms on your travel agency website
-   📧 Email notifications for new form submissions
-   💬 Integrations with tools travel advisors use (Slack, Webhooks, Discord)
-   🧠 Form logic for dynamic travel questionnaires
-   🛡️ Secure client data collection with Captcha protection
-   📊 Form analytics to track client engagement

For a complete list of features and detailed documentation, visit our [Technical Documentation](https://docs.voyagersocial.ai).

## For Travel Advisors

VS Forms is specifically designed for the travel industry:

- **Client Onboarding**: Collect comprehensive client information for your records
- **Travel Preferences**: Build detailed profiles of client travel preferences
- **Trip Planning**: Gather essential details for planning specific trips
- **Feedback Collection**: Learn from clients after their trips to improve service
- **Travel Confirmations**: Get client sign-off on itineraries and bookings
